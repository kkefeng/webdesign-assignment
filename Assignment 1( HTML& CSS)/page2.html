List of More Traditional Festival in Malaysia

Malaysia has a rich melting pot of religious groups, including Muslims, Christians, Buddhist, Hindus and Sikhs and hundreds of different ethnic groups. Because of this, there are multiple events each month. West and East Malaysia often have their separate cultural activities too. Some have open houses where the host puts on a spread of food and invites family and friends. Others involve prayers at the mosque or temple.

  • Wesak Day (May)
  • Kaamatan Harvest Festival in Sabah (30th and 31st May)
  • Gawai Harvest Festival (1st and 2nd June)
  • Rainforest World Music Festival, Kuching, Sarawak (mid-July)
  • Deepavali (November)
